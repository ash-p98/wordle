Ash's Wordle

How to play.

  • Aim of the game is to guess the 5 letter word.
  • You have 6 guesses to get the word.
  • To play click on the input box then type your word.
  • Your guess must be 5 letters long and be in the dictionary.
  • Submit your word with the guess buttom
  • If nothing happens when the button is clicked, your word is invalid, please try a different word.
  • If your word is submited you will see it again.
  • White background= that letter is NOT in the word.
  • Yellow background = In the word but incorrect place.
  • Green background = In word and in correct place
